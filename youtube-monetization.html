YouTube Monetization: Eligibility → Approval → Growth

AdSense‑first blueprint: policies, watch‑time systems, and content packaging that advertisers prefer.

1) Program Eligibility

  • 1,000 subscribers + 4,000 public watch hours (last 12 months) or 10M Shorts views (90 days).
  • Two‑step: YouTube Partner Program + link AdSense account.
  • Keep it clean: copyright, reused content, and policy safety.

2) Content Packaging

  • Clear topic clusters (series) + strong thumbnails.
  • Ad‑friendly categories: tutorials, explainers, highlights with original voiceover.
  • Safe language, safe visuals (avoid graphic/NSFW).

3) Watch‑Time Systems

  • Open with the payoff, then context — “Hooksmith” helps draft cold‑opens.
  • Use mid‑video pattern breaks & CTAs (cards, end screens).
  • Plan a 3‑video arc for bingeability.

4) Monetization Hygiene

  • Original audio track, proper licensing for music/assets.
  • Descriptions with timestamps, links to related videos.
  • Community posts & Shorts to keep recency high.

MBCC Tools That Help

  • Cold‑Open Hooksmith — better first 10 seconds.
  • Shorts Crafter — 9:16 storyboard + captions.
  • Meeting Automator — plan recordings & agendas.
  • File Converter — quick asset conversions.

Next Steps

  • Create a weekly upload plan (2 long + 3 shorts).
  • Batch record, batch edit; schedule releases.
  • Review Analytics → RPM & retention → iterate.
