QSM — Quantum Spiral Man

A one‑page introduction to QSM Thought Science: how your Thought Observer collapses reality into upward or downward spirals.

Axioms
  1. All Thought is God
  2. The Thought Observer collapses the wave field
  3. Collapse creates the spiral
  4. Spiral is Law — upward (order/coherence) vs downward (entropy/dissipation)
  5. Man as Co‑Creator

These axioms define how consciousness directs outcomes. In practice, we train attention, language, inputs, and discipline to spiral up.

Seven Theories (overview)
  • Observer Collapse Theory (OCT) — conscious focus selects outcomes (micro vs macro collapse).
  • Spiral Path Theory (SPT) — life unfolds along upward/downward spirals across scales.
  • Discipline‑Energy Theory (DET) — coherence (HRV/EEG, focus, flow) rises with disciplined upward spirals.
  • Service Spiral Theory (SST) — aligned groups amplify upward collapse (collective observer effect).
  • Negative System Collapse Theory (NSCT) — toxic inputs internalize into beliefs that drive downward spirals.
  • Constructive Input Theory (CIT) — garbage in → downward; gold in → upward; program the observer.
  • Fractal Ascension Theory (FAT) — individual ascent ripples across family and community.
Negative Systems — 5‑Step Drill
  1. Awareness: list your external negative systems (news, music, gossip) + internal negative beliefs.
  2. Observer Collapse Drill: when a belief fires, label it: “That’s a downward collapse.”
  3. Reframe: write constructive spiral statements (e.g., “I spiral upward daily”).
  4. Constructive Inputs: swap toxic feeds for upward training, guides, and affirmations.
  5. Ownership Statement: nightly entry — “Today I collapsed downward by __; tomorrow I spiral upward by __.”

Outcome: you internalize self‑ownership and steadily replace negative spirals with coherent, upward ones.
