49ers Fight Through Injuries to Edge Saints 26-21

Backup QB Mac Jones shines as San Francisco finds a way.

Published September 15, 2025

The San Francisco 49ers showed their depth in a gritty 26-21 victory over the New Orleans Saints.

  • With Brock Purdy and Brandon Aiyuk sidelined, backup QB Mac Jones threw for three touchdowns, proving steady under pressure.
  • The Saints battled hard, fueled by Derek Carr and Alvin Kamara, but late mistakes cost them.
  • A crucial 4th-quarter defensive stand sealed the win for San Francisco.

    • The 49ers improve to 2-0 despite heavy injuries, while the Saints drop a winnable home game that could haunt them later.
