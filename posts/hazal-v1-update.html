Haz‑AL Update: Faster FX, Cleaner Splash, 3‑2‑1 Polish

2025-10-14 • 6 min read

We focused this round on responsiveness and clarity for creators. FX feel snappier, image splash fills better, and 3‑2‑1 recording is cleaner.

What’s Improved

  • FX Responsiveness: Lower input latency and smoother toggling.
  • Image Splash Fit: Better fit for 16:9 and 9:16 without letterboxing.
  • 3‑2‑1 Clarity: Sharper countdown overlay with clear start.

Creator Tips

  • Prep overlays by aspect ratio: 1920×1080 (16:9), 1080×1920 (9:16).
  • Group your go‑to samples in the first row for speed.
  • Use 1–2 FX consistently for style; avoid over-stacking.
