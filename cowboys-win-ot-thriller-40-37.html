Cowboys Win Overtime Thriller Against Giants, 40-37

Brandon Aubrey’s leg lifts Dallas in one of the wildest games of the week.

Published September 15, 2025

The Dallas Cowboys and New York Giants traded blows all night before Dallas escaped with a 40-37 OT victory.

  • There were seven lead changes, making it one of the most entertaining games of the season so far.
  • Dallas kicker Brandon Aubrey drilled the game-tying kick late in the 4th and the game-winner in overtime.
  • Russell Wilson and rookie WR Malik Nabers dazzled for New York, while CeeDee Lamb delivered clutch yardage for Dallas.

    • The Cowboys move to 2-0 with newfound momentum. For the Giants, the loss stings after being so close to a statement win.
