Ravens Roll Over Browns, 41-17, with Explosive 2nd Half

Lamar Jackson’s four TDs fuel Baltimore’s offensive explosion.

Published September 15, 2025

The Cleveland Browns started strong but collapsed in the second half as the Baltimore Ravens stormed to a 41-17 win.

  • Lamar Jackson carved up the defense with four touchdown passes, spreading the ball around masterfully.
  • Baltimore’s defense forced multiple turnovers — a blocked punt, an interception, and a fumble recovery all led to points.
  • Cleveland’s miscues snowballed, leaving Deshaun Watson frustrated and the offense sputtering.

    • With this dominant win, the Ravens send a clear message: they’re early contenders in the AFC. The Browns, meanwhile, need to clean up ball security fast.
