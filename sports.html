  • “NBA on NBC” promo airs during SNF; Opening Night set for Oct 21 on NBC/Peacock.

Sources: NBA.com, AP/FIBA, NBC/Peacock.

Historic moment as Georgia eliminates France 80–70 to reach the quarterfinals for the first time.

Carmelo Anthony, Dwight Howard, Sue Bird, Maya Moore, Sylvia Fowles, Danny Crawford, the 2008 “Redeem Team,” coach Billy Donovan and executive Micky Arison enter the Hall.

Oct 21: Rockets at Thunder (7:30 ET), Warriors at Lakers (10 ET). NBC & Peacock carry Opening Night; promo rolled out during Sunday Night Football.
