NFL Week 1 (2025) — Drop-in Cards
=================================

Files:
- nfl-week1-2025.html
- mc-sports.css

Install:
1) Upload both files alongside your sports.html.
2) Add this in <head> of sports.html (once):
   <link rel="stylesheet" href="mc-sports.css">
3) Paste the html snippet under your NFL section.

Notes:
- Covers Thu–Sun games only (Sep 4–7, 2025).
- Monday (Vikings at Bears) is excluded per your "weekend" request.
- Scores sourced from ESPN's Week 1 scoreboard.
