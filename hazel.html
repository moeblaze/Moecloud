About Hazel

Hazel is your AI video creation tool for MCC. It turns scripts + assets into MCC-styled videos with voiceover options. Use it for Retail Playbooks (e.g., Omnichannel Promotions) or training assets.

Workflow & Compliance

  1. Upload brand pack (logos, fonts, color tokens).
  2. Paste or write your script; choose aspect ratio & style.
  3. Attach product media (images/video) and optional voiceover.
  4. Generate storyboard → review scenes & CTAs.
  5. Render a preview (front‑end only here) or export via API when connected.

Compliance: store assets securely, log consent for any PII. Keep no PII in video metadata.
