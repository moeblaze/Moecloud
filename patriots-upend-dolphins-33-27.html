Patriots Upend Dolphins 33-27 Behind Drake Maye’s Breakout

Rookie QB Drake Maye announces himself with a clutch all-around performance.

Published September 15, 2025

New England fans got a glimpse of the future as rookie QB Drake Maye led the Patriots to a 33-27 victory over the Miami Dolphins.

  • Maye threw two TDs and added a rushing score, outdueling Tua Tagovailoa in crunch time.
  • Special teams delivered a pivotal kickoff return that swung momentum.
  • Miami fought back late, but Maye’s poise under pressure closed the door.

    • The Patriots celebrate a major win that could redefine their season, while Miami looks to rebound after a tough division loss.
